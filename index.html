üîß Technical Infrastructure & Deliverability

Email Authentication Setup

SPF (Sender Policy Framework)
  • Configured to authorize sending servers
  • Prevents email spoofing and impersonation
  • Improves deliverability and trust with ISPs
DKIM (DomainKeys Identified Mail)
  • Digital signatures verify email authenticity
  • Ensures message integrity during transmission
  • Builds sender reputation with inbox providers
DMARC (Domain-based Message Authentication)
  • Policy enforcement for SPF and DKIM
  • Protects brand from phishing and spoofing
  • Provides visibility into email authentication

List Quality Management

Email Validation Process:

  • Syntax validation: Proper email format verification
  • Domain verification: Valid, active domains confirmed
  • Mailbox verification: Deliverable addresses validated
  • Spam trap detection: Identified and removed
  • Duplicate removal: Clean, deduplicated list

Result: 99.3% deliverability with only 0.66% bounce rate
